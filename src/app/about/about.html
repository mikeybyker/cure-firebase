About

An Angular 1.5 (@1.6.0) app with a Firebase backend. View and rate albums/releases by The Cure. These are from my collection - CDs only.
I used Music Collector to scan my CDs and export the data, with a little massaging to get a Firebase friendly denormalized state.
One day I might get round to adding my vinyl etc. too - though that would take a long, long time. I have lots :-)

The CD cover images are as exported from Music Collector - far too many to rename to something nice, so they are what they are.
